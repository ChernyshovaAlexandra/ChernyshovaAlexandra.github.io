How to play?

  • You have to defeat monsters, using different spells
  • Each spell belongs to its own level
  • To apply the spell you need to solve the proposed task correctly
  • Applying of the spell hits the monster
  • Be careful! If you solve the task incorrect the spell will hit not monster, but you!
  • With the raise of level raises the difficulty of the proposed task and the strength of the hit
  • When the monster's health bar become zero, it means that it has bean defeated!
  • Try to defeat all the monsters you meat during the game!
  • Have fun!
